PROGRAM

Flex & Paint

Art & Fitness for Expression & Embodiment

If you struggle with anxiety or disconnection from your body, I want to work with you.

I used to think that I was robotic. I put labels on myself-- anxious, introverted, dissociated. I thought that there were levels to emotion that I wasn't able to feel and that something was irrevocably broken.

I later discovered the practice that I want to share with you-- what I was missing was an embodiment practice and an expression practice.

PROGRAM

8 Weeks of Online Lessons and Exercises

Weekly Group Calls

Community and Accountability

Individual Guidance

Flex and Paint is not quite art therapy, and it isn't quite personal training-- but you'll receive the benefits of both.

By combining an artistic practice and a fitness practice, you're tapping into the mental health benefits of both expression and embodiment.

You don't need to be artistic nor in good shape to get the benefits-- I want to be clear that nothing about Flex and Paint requires you to be fit or arty. You can immediately benefit from expression and embodiment through a series of exercises that will compound on each other.

OUTCOMES

Primary Outcomes

  • Reduce anxiety
  • Combat dissociation
  • Build confidence

Secondary Outcomes

  • Reduced screen addiction
  • Expanded body awareness

Tertiary Outcomes

  • Fundamental fitness habit and early-stage results
  • Foundational artistic practice
