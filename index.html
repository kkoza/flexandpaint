PROGRAM

Flex & Paint

An 8-week program to reduce anxiety, combat dissociation, and reconnect with your body

If you struggle with anxiety or disconnection from your body, I want to work with you.

I used to think that I was robotic. I put labels on myself-- anxious, introverted, dissociated. I thought that there were levels to emotion that I wasn't able to feel and that something was irrevocably broken.

I later discovered the practice that I want to share with you-- what I was missing was an embodiment practice and an expression practice.

THE METHOD

Most people try fitness OR art separately. That's not enough.

Here's what I discovered: when you alternate between physical movement and creative expression in the same session, something unique happens. You enter a flow state where you're entirely present in your body—no anxious thoughts, no dissociation, just pure embodied experience.

Fitness alone keeps you in your head, counting reps and tracking metrics. Art alone can become cerebral and disconnected. But together? They create a practice that pulls you completely into the present moment.

This isn't art therapy. This isn't personal training. It's both—and the combination is what makes it work.

PROGRAM

8 Weeks of Online Lessons and Exercises

Weekly Group Calls

Community and Accountability

Individual Guidance

Over 8 weeks, you'll build a sustainable practice that compounds. Weekly group calls keep you accountable. A supportive community reminds you that you're not alone. And personalized guidance ensures you're making real progress.

You don't need to be fit or artistic to start. The exercises are designed for complete beginners who just want to feel connected to themselves again.
